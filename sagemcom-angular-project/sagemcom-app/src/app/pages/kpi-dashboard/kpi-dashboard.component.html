About this Metric

Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE/TRG) evaluates how effectively a manufacturing operation is utilized. It is calculated as Availability × Performance × Quality.

First Pass Yield (FPY) measures the percentage of products manufactured correctly the first time without any rework or scrap. It is a strict indicator of process quality.

Availability measures the percentage of planned production time that the equipment is actually operating and producing parts, taking into account machine downtime and setups.

Performance Rate measures how close the equipment is running to its theoretical maximum speed while it is operating, highlighting micro-stops or slow cycles.

Production Volume indicates the total number of physical units produced across the selected timeframe and production lines.

This metric tracks performance against predefined target thresholds to ensure operational excellence.
